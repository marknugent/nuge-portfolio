My role: Visual design

My employer, SiFive, was involved with various open-source projects related to the semiconductor industry. A colleague asked if I would design logos for two projects: TileLink, a standard for interconnections between various regions of a microchip, and Rocket Chip, an open-source CPU core design generator.

Logo design isn't something I'm really focused on, but I had fun doing these and I think I did a decent enough job.
