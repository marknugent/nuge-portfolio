House Plans Sites

Product: E-commerce website

Role: Visual design

Hanley Wood has a portfolio of several brands for essentially the same underlying e-commerce site for selling house plans. They are differentiated by their visual design. I was asked to produce designs for two new websites, BuilderHousePlans and Select Home Designs.

BuilderHousePlans was to follow the style of the Builder Online website, Hanley Wood's trade publication for the construction industry. Select Home Designs had no existing design guidelines beyond its logo and color scheme, so I devised an original style for this mockup.

These were produced around 2008, a year or so after the first iPhone came to market, and responsive design hadn't yet gained currency. The layouts are representative of the style of the day — dense, complex desktop designs.

The management seemed happy with these designs, but ultimately shelved the project. All of Hanley Wood's house plans sites were soon transitioned to a new, simplified template.
