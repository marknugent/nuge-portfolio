Hudson Institute Kleptocracy Infographic

My role: Visual design, HTML/CSS, JS

Background

The Kleptocracy Initiative, a Hudson Institute project, wanted to convey through an interactive infographic how Chinese political leaders amass fortunes and hide them behind family networks and shadowy business groups.

Using written materials provided by Hudson Institute staff, I created mockups in Sketch and produced the infographic using HTML, Javsascript/jquery, and CSS 3 animations. I also copy edited the accompanying articles and wrote the infographic’s headline and subhed.

This was my first project of this sort, and as I look back on it I may have done some things differently, but the graphic was well received.

The graphic can be seen here.

Hudson was interested in doing a follow-up infographic centering on Zhou Yongkang, a Communist Party leader who was imprisoned for corruption. I produced a mockup but had to leave the project due to time constraints.
